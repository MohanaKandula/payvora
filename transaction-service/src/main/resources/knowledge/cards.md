# PayVora Virtual Cards & Sandbox Simulations

## Overview
PayVora allows you to generate and manage virtual cards instantly. Virtual cards are linked to your Spendable Wallet and can be used for zero-fee card simulations.

## Generating Virtual Cards
- **Instant Creation**: Navigate to the Virtual Cards section on your dashboard and click "Create Card" to generate a unique 16-digit card number, CVV, and expiration date.
- **Card Skins**: Customize your virtual cards by choosing different visual themes or credit skins.

## Card Security
- **Freeze/Unfreeze**: Instantly toggle the freeze state of a card to block all transactions if you suspect unauthorized activity.
- **Termination**: Permanently delete a card if you no longer need it.

## Sandboxed Card Payments
- You can simulate debit payments or refund reversals using your virtual cards to test application logic and ledger entries.
- Transaction events are posted to your double-entry Spendable Wallet with immediate ledger clearance.
