# PayVora Member Account & Profile Structure

## Overview
Your PayVora Account represents your digital banking profile, linking all your spendable wallets, yield vaults, identity verification (KYC), and security settings.

## Account Features
- **Account Identifier**: Unique 12-digit digital bank account number and UUID assigned upon registration.
- **Linked External Accounts**: Ability to link external bank accounts or debit cards for deposits and withdrawals.
- **Profile Management**: Update contact details, mailing address, notification preferences, and tax documentation.
- **Multi-Wallet Support**: Your single PayVora account credentials provide access to your Spendable Wallet, Savings Vault, and Rewards Wallet.
