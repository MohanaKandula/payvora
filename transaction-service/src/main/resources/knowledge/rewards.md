# Cashback & Rewards Hub
## Overview
The Rewards page (`/rewards`) allows members to view active promotion offers, track accumulated cashback, redeem earnings 1:1, and manage their referral network.

## Core Rewards Balances
- **Available Cashback**: Current promotional cashback balance available for redemption.
- **Total Cashback Earned**: Lifetime rewards accumulated from cashbacks and referrals.
- **Cashback Redeemed**: Cumulative rewards successfully transferred out to the Spendable Wallet.

## Page Features & Widgets
1. **Available Cashback Offers**: Displays active promotional campaigns (such as Grocery Boost or Mobile Recharge offers) with their minimum transaction amount and reward details.
2. **Loyalty Invitation Program (Referrals)**:
   - Displays the member's unique Referral Code.
   - **My Invites Modal**: Tracks referred joiners, displaying their names, usernames, KYC statuses (Pending/Approved/Failed), and indicates if the **$10.00 cash bonus** was credited (credited instantly upon the referee's KYC Approval).
3. **Redeem to Wallet**:
   - A manual form allowing members to transfer their available cashback balance 1:1 into their primary Spendable Wallet.
   - **Requirement**: Member's identity verification (KYC) must be in an `APPROVED` status to execute a redemption.
