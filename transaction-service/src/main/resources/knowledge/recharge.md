# PayVora Bill Payments & Mobile Recharges

## Overview
PayVora allows you to pay utility bills, recharge prepaid mobile lines, and settle household services directly from your Spendable Wallet.

## Supported Bill & Recharge Services
- **Mobile Prepaid & Postpaid**: Instant mobile top-ups across major telecommunication carriers.
- **Utility Bills**: Electricity, Water, Piped Gas, and Municipal tax payments.
- **Entertainment & Connectivity**: DTH Satellite TV and High-speed Broadband internet bills.

## Earning Cashback on Recharges
- Eligible utility bill payments and mobile recharges earn automatic cashback rebates when active promotional campaigns are enabled.
- Payments process instantly from your Spendable Wallet with immediate digital receipts.
