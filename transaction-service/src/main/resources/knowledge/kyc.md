# PayVora Identity Verification (KYC) Policy

## Overview
To comply with banking regulations and prevent financial fraud, PayVora requires all account holders to complete Know Your Customer (KYC) identity verification.

## Verification Requirements
- **Government Document**: Clear photo upload of a valid Driver's License, Passport, or National ID.
- **Identity Information**: Full legal name, date of birth, Social Security Number (SSN) or Tax ID, and residential address.
- **Name Matching**: The legal name on your identity document must exactly match your registered PayVora account name.

## Common Reasons for KYC Rejection
1. **Name Mismatch**: Legal document name differs from registered PayVora profile name.
2. **Unclear Image**: Uploaded document photo is blurry, cropped, dark, or unreadable.
3. **Expired Document**: Submitted government ID is past its expiration date.

## Resolution
If your KYC verification was rejected, review the rejection reason in your Profile Settings, update any name mismatches, and re-upload a clear, un-cropped photo of your valid ID.
