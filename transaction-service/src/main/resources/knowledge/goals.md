# PayVora Savings Goals & Target Tracking

## Overview
PayVora Savings Goals empower members to set up dedicated financial targets (e.g., Emergency Fund, Vacation, New Car) with visual progress tracking and automated savings rules.

## Features
- **Goal Setup**: Specify a goal title, target savings amount, and target completion date.
- **Goal Progress**: Real-time progress bar displaying percentage completed towards your savings goal.
- **Monthly Savings Suggestions**: Automated calculations suggesting how much to save monthly or weekly to achieve your target on time.
- **Goal Editing & Withdrawals**: Adjust target amounts, change completion dates, or transfer savings back to your Spendable Wallet at any time.

## Creating a Savings Goal
1. Navigate to **Savings Goals** on your dashboard.
2. Click **Create New Goal**, enter your target amount and date.
3. Transfer funds from your Spendable Wallet to start building your goal balance.
