# PayVora Account Statements & History PDF Export

## Overview
PayVora allows members to generate, view, and export official monthly bank statements and detailed transaction activity logs.

## Features
- **Download PDF Statement**: Export official monthly account statements formatted with opening balance, itemized debits/credits, earned interest, and closing balance.
- **Statement Periods**: Select any past calendar month or custom date range.
- **Transaction Search & Filters**: Search transaction history by keywords, categories, or transaction IDs.
- **CSV & PDF Export**: Download raw CSV transaction data or official PDF bank statements instantly.

## Downloading Statements
1. Navigate to **Account Statements** or open the Statements tab under your profile.
2. Choose the desired month and format (PDF or CSV).
3. Click **Generate Statement** to download immediately.
