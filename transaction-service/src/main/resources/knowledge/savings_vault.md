# PayVora Savings Vault & Yield Engine

## Overview
The PayVora Savings Vault is an automated high-yield account where member balances earn interest calculated daily and compounded automatically.

## How Savings Vault Earns Interest
- **Dynamic APY**: Interest accrues at the current Annual Percentage Yield (APY) rate configured in PayVora Investment Settings.
- **Daily Accrual**: Interest is calculated daily at midnight (00:00 UTC) based on your end-of-day Vault balance.
- **Automatic Compounding**: Earned interest payouts compound directly into your Savings Vault principal balance.

## Deposits & Withdrawals
- Funds can be transferred to or from your Spendable Wallet at any time without penalty.
- There are no minimum lockup periods unless participating in a specialized fixed-term yield lock.
