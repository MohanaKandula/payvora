# PayVora Cashback Rewards & Rebate Policy

## Overview
PayVora rewards members with automatic cashback credits on eligible merchant transactions, grocery purchases, utility bill recharges, and promotional partner spending.

## Earning Cashback
- **Merchant Categories**: Eligible transactions across Groceries, Rent Payments, Mobile Recharges, and DTH/Utility Bills earn percentage rebates as specified in active promotion campaigns.
- **Credit Timeline**: Approved cashback credits are automatically posted to your Reward Wallet upon transaction settlement.
- **Posting Requirements**: Transactions must be completed successfully and pass security fraud verification filters.

## Troubleshooting Delayed Cashback
If cashback was not received for an eligible payment:
1. Verify that the transaction status is marked as **COMPLETED** (pending or failed transfers do not qualify).
2. Confirm that the transaction category matches an active PayVora cashback offer.
3. Check if your monthly cashback earning limit for the specific promotional tier has been reached.
