# PayVora Wallet & Multi-Wallet Architecture

## Overview
PayVora provides a multi-wallet architecture designed for seamless daily spending, automated savings, and reward earning under a single bank account.

## Wallet Types
1. **Spendable Wallet**: Primary checking wallet used for P2P money transfers, debit purchases, utility bill payments, and mobile recharges.
2. **Savings Vault**: Dedicated yield-generating wallet where funds earn daily compounding interest at the active APY rate configured by PayVora.
3. **Reward & Cashback Wallet**: Dedicated promotional wallet where merchant cashback rebates and referral bonuses accumulate.

## Wallet Balances
- **Available Balance**: Real-time liquid funds immediately accessible for transfers, bills, and withdrawals.
- **Total Balance**: Sum of your Spendable Wallet, active Savings Vault balances, and accrued cashback rewards.

## Multiple Wallets Policy
- Every PayVora member is automatically provisioned with a Spendable Wallet, Savings Vault, and Cashback Wallet.
- Internal transfers between your own Spendable Wallet and Savings Vault occur instantly with zero transfer fees.
